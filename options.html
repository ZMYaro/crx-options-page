Pop-up pages

An pop-up page should have a class of page, and it should be inside an element of class overlay. The .page is the pane itself, and the .overlay is the white background that covers the main page and, when clicked, hides the overlay pane.

To show a pop-up page, call [page element].show(). To hide it, call [page element].fadeOut(). You can also use [page element].hide() to hide it instantly.

If you do not intend to use pop-up pages or close buttons, you can exclude the options-widgets.css file from your project.
